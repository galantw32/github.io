About Will Galant

Will Galant founded 427 Capital with the goal of acquiring and operating a small business. Upon closing, he is committed to leading the company with the same dedication and vision as its previous owner, ensuring its continued success and long-term growth.

Background

Entrepreneurship has always been in Will's DNA. Growing up, he watched his father build and run successful public and private businesses, inspiring a deep appreciation for leadership, employee well-being, and customer satisfaction. While in college, Will launched a startup, winning multiple business model and pitch competitions before selling his stake to his partner to pursue a career in finance.

Professional Experience

  • Invested in founder-led businesses at a lower middle-market private equity firm in Dallas, specializing in industrials and business services.
  • Worked as a management consultant at McKinsey & Company, advising clients on strategic growth and operational improvements across various industries.

Education

Will holds a double major in Finance and Management from Tulane University, with a minor in Psychology.
